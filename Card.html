Ash and Pikachu

The most famous Pokémon is Pikachu, known for its cute appearance and electric powers. The main human character in the anime is Ash Ketchum (known as Satoshi in Japan), who travels with Pikachu and friends across different regions. Other well-known Pokémon include Charizard, Bulbasaur, Squirtle, and Eevee.

Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main character of the anime and manga series One Piece, created by Eiichiro Oda. He is a cheerful, brave, and adventurous young pirate who dreams of finding the legendary treasure called “One Piece” and becoming the Pirate King.

Tanjiro

Tanjiro Kamado is the main character of the anime and manga Demon Slayer. He is a kind, humble, and determined boy who comes from a poor but loving family. His life changes when demons attack his home and kill most of his family, leaving only his younger sister Nezuko, who is turned into a demon.

Shinchan

Shinchan is playful, naughty, and often makes silly jokes that cause trouble for his parents, teachers, and friends. He loves to tease people with his funny dance moves and strange remarks. Even though he is very mischievous, he is also innocent and caring in his own way.

Doraemon

Doraemon has a special 4D pocket on his stomach, from which he can take out amazing futuristic gadgets. Some of the most famous ones are the Anywhere Door (a door that can take you anywhere), the Time Machine, and the Bamboo Copter (a small propeller that lets people fly). These gadgets usually help Nobita, but sometimes cause funny and unexpected troubles.
